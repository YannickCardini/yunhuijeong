Biography

I am a dedicated PhD candidate at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, specializing in Applied Linguistics. My research focuses on the intersection of language, technology, and pedagogy. I am passionate about understanding how digital tools can enhance language acquisition, particularly in the context of second language learning. My academic journey has equipped me with a strong theoretical foundation and practical research skills.
